Induction-Coupling Architecture (ICA)

IDL • IAS

ICA is a computing architecture that organizes deterministic logic and adaptive, brain-inspired computation around controlled electromagnetic induction coupling.

Core components:
  • IDL – Induction-Based Digital Logic
  • IAS – Induction-Based Adaptive Synapse
Research focus:
  • Alternative computing architectures
  • Brain-inspired hardware
  • Inductive computing primitives
Status:

Working hardware prototypes demonstrated. Documentation and videos will be added.
